About Us

The Bank Of The Sun was founded in 2017 by our co-finders Alexander Cawood and Alexander Powell. They studied together at the University of Stellenbosch and had the idea of opening a bank.

The idea came from mainly places, but the main one, was how they struggled to find jobs after their graduation. The idea was to build a bank catered around the students of Stellenbosch. They partnered up with the local businesses around them to provide internships for students, and if they prosper, jobs.

Our main aim is to provide exclusive service to our clients and help build our future.
